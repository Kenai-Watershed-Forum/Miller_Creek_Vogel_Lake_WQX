Miller Creek and Vogel Lake Water Quality

2 Lake Water Quality Profiles

Lake water quality profiles were collected at 1-2 month intervals at five sites at 1 meter depth intervals. Hydrolab MS5 sondes were descended from an anchored boat centered over the deepest point of each lake, and parameter values were recorded from a handheld reader once stabilized after 30-60 seconds.

Raw water quality field data is stored in a Google Sheet that can be viewed at https://tinyurl.com/kwf-vogel-wqx-data.

Data visualizations are provided for each site visit here.
